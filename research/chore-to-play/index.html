Chore to Play

Visualizing the collapse and reconstruction of a theory.

ORIGINAL HYPOTHESIS

처음에는 계단이라고 생각했습니다.

We naturally assume that work evolves linearly. From the mundane to the meaningful, a step-by-step ascension towards fulfillment. The progression from chore to calling seems logical, an inevitable journey of personal and professional growth.

하지만 실제 삶은 그렇게 움직이지 않았습니다.

The words belong to different categories. They are not steps to climb, but layers that coexist within a single individual at any given moment.

EXAMPLE: RESTAURANT MANAGER

Consider the restaurant manager. They hold a Job and inhabit a Role. They execute Chores (sweeping) and Tasks (inventory). They bear Duty (compliance) and Responsibility (staff wellbeing). They practice Craft (hospitality). All these layers exist simultaneously.

Duty

The obligations imposed by structure and necessity. What must be done.

Job

The economic exchange. The container of time and effort for reward.

Role

The relational identity within a system. How one is seen and expected to act.

Responsibility

The ownership of outcomes. The moral or practical burden of consequences.
